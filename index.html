Case Study

Cushy Cuisine
Telegram Bot

Automated Ordering. Zero Friction. 100% Digital.

Cushy Cuisine is a fast-growing Nigerian restaurant brand delivering comfort food across Ile-Ife and environs. I designed and built a fully automated Telegram Bot that transformed their manual WhatsApp ordering process into a seamless digital experience — handling orders, managing a digital wallet system, and automatically building their customer database. All without a single human in the loop.

The System

One bot. Every order. Zero manual work.

1

Customer opens Telegram

The customer finds Cushy Cuisine on Telegram and starts the bot with a single tap.

2

Automated menu presented

The bot presents the full menu with prices, pack options, and extras — exactly as designed. The customer selects their order through button taps, not typing.

3

Digital wallet & payment

The customer's digital wallet is checked or topped up. Payment is confirmed within the bot — no redirects, no confusion.

4

Order logged & kitchen notified

The confirmed order is instantly written to Google Sheets, the customer receives a confirmation, and the kitchen team sees a clean, structured order. No phone calls. No WhatsApp voice notes. No errors.
