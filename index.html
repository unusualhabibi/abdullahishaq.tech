Case Study 01 — OAU Campus, Nigeria
Flora Scents — From WhatsApp chaos to fully automated operation.
Flora Scents is a perfume vendor at OAU campus running over 200 products. Every order was manual — DMs, mental tracking, handwritten notes. I built a complete business infrastructure: inventory system across all 200+ SKUs, an automated order intake flow, 12 WhatsApp quick replies handling every customer question, a receipt generator, a scent catalogue with descriptions and gender tags, and a 30-caption content bank. The owner now does three things: verify payment, answer escalated questions, and hand orders to the rider. Everything else runs without her.
