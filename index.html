I designed and built a fully automated Telegram Bot that transformed their manual WhatsApp ordering process into a seamless digital experience — handling orders, managing a digital wallet system, and automatically building their customer database. All without a single human in the loop.

The System

One bot. Every order. Zero manual work.

01

Customer opens Telegram

The customer finds Cushy Cuisine on Telegram and starts the bot with a single tap.

02

Automated menu presented

The bot presents the full menu with prices, pack options, and extras. The customer selects their order through button taps, not typing.

03

Digital wallet + payment

The customer's digital wallet is checked or topped up. Payment is confirmed within the bot — no redirects, no confusion.

04

Order logged + kitchen notified

The confirmed order is instantly written to Google Sheets, the customer receives a confirmation, and the kitchen team sees a clean, structured order. No phone calls. No errors.

Under the Hood

Built with the right tools for the job.

PythonAnywhere

The bot runs 24/7 on PythonAnywhere's cloud infrastructure — always online, always ready, zero downtime for the restaurant.

Telegram Bot API

Telegram's Bot API powers the entire customer-facing experience — rich menus, inline buttons, wallet interactions, and instant confirmations.

Google Sheets Database

Every order, every customer, every transaction — logged in real time to a structured Google Sheets database. Simple for the restaurant team to read, powerful enough to build reports from.
